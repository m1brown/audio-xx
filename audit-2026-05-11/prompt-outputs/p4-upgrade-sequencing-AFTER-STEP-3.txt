Sequencing question noted — you're asking whether to upgrade DAC, speakers first.

Sequencing depends on which part of the chain is currently most-bottlenecked, not on which category is "most upgradeable" generically.

A few things shape the answer:

• What does the current system already do well? Upgrading a category that already serves its purpose tends to deliver smaller returns than upgrading the bottleneck.
• What about the current sound do you want to change?
• Speakers are usually the hardest to undo; DACs are usually the easiest. Reversibility matters when sequencing.

Can you share the current components (brand and model for each) and what you most want to change about how the system sounds? With that I can be specific about the order.
