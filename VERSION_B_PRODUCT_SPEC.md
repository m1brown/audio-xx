Audio XX is a system-aware listening advisor. It helps people make better long-term audio decisions by reasoning about how components interact in a specific system.

The product combines: engineering and topology understanding, system interaction reasoning, structured sonic-trait modeling, contextual product knowledge, and listener preference interpretation.

It answers: "Is this actually right for this listener, this system, and this goal?" — not "What product is best?"

Knowledge sources: design topology and manufacturer specs, listening consensus from communities, user context (system, room, preferences), personal listening experience for anchor products.

Core use cases: preventing unnecessary upgrades, diagnosing listener fatigue, understanding personal listening preferences, system assessment, comparison with trade-off framing.
